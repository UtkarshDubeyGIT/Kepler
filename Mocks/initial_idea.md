# Initial Idea

- Ability to create schedule on voice commands, after some questions asked, requirements as per the user. sleeping habits and usual daily tasks ingrained
- Ability to change the schedule as per tasks and delays etc.
- Different Horizons for schedule i.e. Long / short / today / day-wise / month-wise.
- Alarms and reminders for activities
- integrations with other productivity and work apps like google suit, notion, todos, mail, jira, etc.
